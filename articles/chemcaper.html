Why You’ll Love It

ChemCaper turns chemistry concepts into a fantasy RPG adventure. You can learn chemistry without feeling like you’re studying. Instead you explore worlds, fight enemies, collect chemical elements, and craft compounds.

Scientific Concepts Explored

  • Elements & the Periodic Table: Discover chemical elements represented as characters.
  • Chemical Bonding: Learn how compounds form through ionic and covalent bonding by crafting items and solving puzzles.
  • Molecular Structures: Understand how atoms combine into molecules through gameplay interactions.
  • Chemical Reactions: Quests involve making substances, creating new compounds, and seeing reaction outcomes.
  • Applied Chemistry: Concepts like pH, acids/bases, and real-life uses appear naturally in the story progression.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand basic chemistry concepts through visual, interactive gameplay.
  • Identify elements, compounds, and bonds with character-based metaphors.
  • Strengthen problem solving and reasoning through themed quests and puzzles.
  • Gain confidence in chemistry by learning through exploration and experimentation.

Gameplay Overview

ChemCaper is a story based RPG where you play as a young character in a fantasy world. You explore regions, talk to characters who represent chemical elements, fight enemies using chemistry based powers, and craft items by combining substances. As you progress, you learn chemistry, from atoms and bonds to reactions and compounds, all while playing.

History & Fun Facts

  • Founded in 2016 by ACE EdVenture Studio, a Malaysian team focused on making education more fun.
  • It is the world’s first chemistry role playing game (RPG).
  • The game’s characters (“Moppets”) are designed based on real elements, each with unique personalities inspired by their scientific properties.
  • ChemCaper was created with the help of teachers and students.
  • It won multiple awards for innovation in education and serious gaming.

Easter Eggs

ChemCaper contains hidden references to real chemical properties, bonus dialogues unlocked by interacting repeatedly with element characters, and secret items that represent rare compounds.
